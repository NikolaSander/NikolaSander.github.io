Paper in Science

In 2014, every scientist's dream came true when the leading journal Science accepted the manuscript by Guy Abel and me for publication. The paper titled "Quantifying Global International Migration Flows" presented international migration flows between all countries in the world for the very first time. The paper was accompanied by an interactive data visualisation of the migration flow estimated presented in the paper. The publication generated an incredible amount of media attention and had quite an impact on my scientific career.
