• Data Visualisation Project — 2023

The Global Flow of People 2.0

This visualisation let's you explore where people move to and from in the world. You can switch between international migration, refugees, and internal migration in Germany. The project was done together with Andreas Genoni and Tamilwai Kolowa from the BiB, Tina Frank and Clemens Schrammel from the University of Arts Linz, and Ramon Bauer from Statistics Vienna. You can find the full version at the BiB's website. On the website, we provide more download material including data and R scripts.

This visualisation is based on an earlier version that I published together with colleagues from the Vienna Institute of Demography in 2014 at www.global-migration.info.
