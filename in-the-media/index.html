Our Research in the Media

The level of media attention our research on The Global Flow of People has received since Guy Abel and I published our paper Quantifying Global International Migration Flows in Science on March 28 is absolutely mind blowing!

Like most success stories, The Global Flow of People has been a fantastic group effort by me, Ramon Bauer, Guy Abel, Null2 and Elvira Stein. Thanks also to the Wittgenstein Centre for Demography and Global Human Capital and the Vienna Institute of Demography for their support.

Here’s a selection of all the stories and blog posts that have popped up across the globe.

  • Where everyone in the world is migrating—in one gorgeous chart; Quartz, by Nick Stockton, published March 27

  • Global Migrant Flows: An Interactive Map – Scientific American, by Geoffrey Giller, published March 28

  • Rising tide of migration is a myth, say global stats; New Scientist, by Jon White, published March 27

  • Research duo quantify global human migration numbers; Phys.org, by Bob Yirka, published March 28

  • Visualizing Which Countries People Are Trying To Get Away From, And Where They’re Going; Fast Company Co.Exist, by Jessica Leber, published March 27

  • 20 Years of Global Migration—in One Chart; The Atlantic, by Nick Stockton, published March 28

  • Da dove vengono e dove vanno i migranti; Internationale, March 31

  • Die Welt ist konstant in Bewegung; Spiegel Online, March 28

  • Globale Migration seit 1990 stabil; Science ORF, March 28

  • Neue Studie: Globale Migration seit 1990 nahezu unverändert; Der Standard, March 27

  • Auf der Spur der Migranten; Bild der Wissenschart, March 28

  • Tutte le migrazioni degli ultimi 20 anni; Rai, March 31
