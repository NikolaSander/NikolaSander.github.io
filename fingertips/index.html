The Global Flow of People at Your Fingertips

Our new interactive data visualisation available at global-migration.info offers you unique information on international migration flows at your fingertips. Coded by Null2 using d3.js, our tool called The Global Flow of People allows the public, policy-makers, and scientists to explore for themselvesÂ where everyone was migrating in the world between 1990 and 2010. Give it a try! Hover your mouse over the graphic below, click on a coloured segment of the circle, and start exploring!

The Global Flow of People is a group effort by me, Guy Abel, Ramon Bauer, Johannes Schmidt, Andi Pieper and Elvira Stein.

The data shown in this visualisation is available in the supplementary materials of our paper published in Science, which can be accessed via global-migration.info with no paywall.
